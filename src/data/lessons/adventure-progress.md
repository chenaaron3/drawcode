# Adventure Progress

Keep track of your hero's accomplishments! Progress tracking makes adventures feel rewarding and helps players understand how far they've come.

## Progress Variables

Track different types of achievements:

```python
quest_name = 'The Lost Crown'
rooms_explored = 5
monsters_defeated = 2
treasures_found = 1
quest_complete = False
```

## Progress Report

Display achievements clearly:

```python
print('=== QUEST PROGRESS ===')
print('Current Quest:', quest_name)
print('Rooms explored:', rooms_explored)
print('Monsters defeated:', monsters_defeated)
print('Treasures found:', treasures_found)
print('Quest complete:', quest_complete)
```

## Types of Progress

Adventures typically track:
- **Exploration**: Rooms, areas, or maps discovered
- **Combat**: Enemies defeated or battles won
- **Collection**: Items, treasures, or artifacts found
- **Story**: Quests completed or NPCs met

## Progress Psychology

Tracking progress:
- **Motivates players**: "I've defeated 10 monsters!"
- **Shows growth**: "I've explored half the dungeon"
- **Creates goals**: "I need 3 more treasures to win"
- **Builds satisfaction**: "Quest complete!"

## Achievement Systems

Many adventures use progress to:
- Unlock new areas or abilities
- Display leaderboards or rankings
- Award special titles or rewards
- Create completion percentages

## Key Concept

Progress tracking transforms random activities into meaningful achievements. By remembering what players have accomplished, adventures create a sense of growth and accomplishment.
